Engineering Highlights

Hardware Architecture

Powered by Microchip ATmega328P-AU. Features a dedicated ICSP header and robust decoupling with 100nF capacitors adjacent to VCC and AVCC.

PCB Engineering

Optimized 2-layer routing strategy with a solid ground plane for EMI reduction, controlled trace widths, and clear silkscreen labeling.

Power Subsystem

Dual power inputs via VIN and USB, featuring a stable 5V regulated rail optimized to provide clean power for embedded applications.
